About Us

Arkansas Movie Databsae is a final project for my Capstone Class at University of Arkansas at Little Rock. This site showcases all movies and actors that has anything to do with cinema in Arkansas, whether the movie or scene is made in Arkansas or if the actors are born and/or raised in Arkansas.

All movies and actors on this site has been selected from the Encyclopedia of Arkansas. All movie and actor data are pulled straight from the API on The Movie Database.
